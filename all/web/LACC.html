Latin American Corruption Crusade

A website created for educational purposes among North American audiences concerning issues regarding corruption within Latin American society. The site looks into governmental, social, and economic issues that specific countries may face, though these topics are also examined on a broader scale to open discussions about similarities and differences within the region's nations.

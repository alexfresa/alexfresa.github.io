1 Sweet Day

Visual elements of the performance consist of a background projection, a contact microphone, and a large tub filled with unknown objects. The projection plays a video of live bees that creates a delayed buzzing sound throughout the performance. As the tub is rummaged through, the projection behind it becomes distorted in response to the microphone. The performance ends as a specific object is found within the tub.

Conceptually, this performance analyzes the displacement of space and how it remains occupied in every sense of the word. Whether this space is occupied sonically, physically, or visually, it is not truly our own and is always shared to varying degrees. The reaction between the contact mic and the projection work to illustrate this "disturbance".

Thin Skin

Site specific installation attempting to personify the tree that it was built around. The piece primarily consists of burlap and tissue. The burlap was shredded and burned in various places to illusrate a wounded organism. The added red dye also accents this idea. Glue wash was used to adhere the fabric to the tree and grants the piece its shape.

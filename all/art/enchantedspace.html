  • ENCHANTED SPACE

    • An interactive installation that utilizes elements of sculpture and VR. The piece's conceptual goal was to create a unique and disorienting space that existied both virtually and physically. Though the space had spiritual influences and undertones, it was not explicitly made out to resemble a religious experience.

    The physical space acted somewhat as a doorway into the virtual space as this is where participants were equipped with a virtual reality headset. The virtual space was designed to resemble the shape of its physical counterpart, though its structures and various visual effects still separated the two greatly.

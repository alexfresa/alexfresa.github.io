Playthings

Sculpture series inspired by the modern horrors of AI and the uncanny valley. These Sculptures, Mommy (right) and Daddy (left) stand as manifestations of these fears, containing both playful and daunting physical elements that work to mask their purpose. Both pieces were constructed using welded 1/4" wire frames wrapped in plastic bags. Mommy has an additional outer frame padded with foam.

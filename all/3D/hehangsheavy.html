He Hangs Heavy

A life-size sculpture created as a personal take on the appearance of a traditional scarecrow.

Strips of burlap were used to contain the sticks and hay used to stuff the head. The sculpture's foundation consists of two crossed boards of stained wood. The vertical board is spiked so that it may be driven into the ground, which holds the sculpture upright. When the wind blows, the scarecrow sways from side to side.

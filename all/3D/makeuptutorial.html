Makeup Tutorial

Interactive installation created to examine the feeling of sensory deprivation and the relationship between the body and its senses. Users were prompted to sit down in front of a screen and put on the pair of headphones presented to them. To their right, an accompanying sensor responds to any movements they make. As the video of an ambiguous figure proceeds to seemingly interact with the user, their discomfort and curiosity are challenged. When the user moves, one of many disturbing or alarming sounds is triggered and can be heard in the headphones.

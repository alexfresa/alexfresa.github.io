LA DIAVOLESSA: CASSIE'S CORRUPTION

Final submission for UNCA's video art course. Students were given thirty seconds to create a fully developed atmosphere using stop motion animation techniques. A collection of paper cut-outs were superimposed over borrowed footage to enhance this effect. This production worked to create an unnerving intensity in the viewer between its unrelenting soundtrack and its abrupt yet grim visual cuts.

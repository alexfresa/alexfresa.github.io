noKnow - "A contemporary faux-informational site that works to reflect a state of digitalized dissociation. Within its initially believable interface, noKnow publishes content that demonstrates abstraction and juxtaposition in its process and reads in a way that is almost unintelligible."

Misconnections - A web-reliant artwork that utilizes non-linear navigation through a series of disorienting interactive pages. Each page is loosely based upon a different state of the human psyche and works to evoke a user reaction by enveloping them in a similar sensory experience.
